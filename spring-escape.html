ABOUT

Spring Escape is a 2D platformer created by Electro, with music by bouncytorch, where you take control of a little patch of dirt and dig your way through various obstacle-filled levels.
Hide from deadly cameras, avoid pesky drills, and so much more.

Alongside the main campaign, the game also features collectibles that let you unlock even harder challenges, a speedrun mode for the fastest of players, and a lot of options to customize the game to your liking.
